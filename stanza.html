I worked with Stanza Living on a freelance project as the lead product designer, helped improve the user experience for a number of internal tools and consumer facing apps.

My role was to discuss the user insights with product managers and turn the basic wireframes, provided to me based on the user research and business requirements into full fledged mockups, create prototypes with those mockups and test them with actual users.

How Feetapart works
